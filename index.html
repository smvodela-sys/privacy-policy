Privacy Policy

Effective date: 21 May 2026  ·  Applies to all apps published by smvodela
🔒
Your data stays on your device.

All apps published under the smvodela developer account store your content — including photos, notes, goals, journal entries, and personal settings — exclusively on your device. We do not run servers that receive or store your personal data, and we do not require you to create an account.

1. Who We Are

This policy covers all mobile applications published by smvodela on the Apple App Store and Google Play Store. Each app may have a slightly different feature set, but they all share the same core privacy principles described in this document.

If you have a question specific to a particular app, please use the contact details in section 8.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, process, or store any of the following on any external server:

  • Your name, email address, or any account credentials
  • Photos, images, or videos you select or capture inside the app
  • Text you write (notes, journal entries, affirmations, goals)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs through our own systems
  • Contacts, calendars, or health data

3. Data Stored on Your Device

All content you create within our apps — boards, images, entries, tasks, preferences, and settings — is stored locally in your device's private app storage. This data is:

  • Private by default — accessible only by the app itself on your device
  • Not backed up to our servers — iCloud or Google Drive backups are controlled entirely by your device settings, not by us
  • Deleted when you uninstall — removing the app permanently deletes all locally stored data

4. Device Permissions

Depending on the app, some or all of the following permissions may be requested. Each permission is only requested when the relevant feature is used:

Permission Why it is needed
Camera optional Lets you take a photo directly inside the app. The image is saved to your device only and is never uploaded.
Photo Library optional Lets you choose an existing image from your gallery to use within the app. We read only the image you select; your full library is not accessed.
Notifications optional Lets the app send you a local daily reminder that you configure yourself. No notification content is transmitted to any server.
Internet optional Used only where an app feature requires it (see section 5). Never used to transmit your personal content or media.

You may revoke any permission at any time in your device's Settings app. Revoking a permission disables only the associated feature; the rest of the app continues to function normally.

5. Internet & API Connections

Most of our apps work entirely offline. Where an app does connect to the internet, the connection falls into one of the following categories:

  • Advertising — Some free apps display ads served by AppLovin MAX. AppLovin may collect a device advertising identifier (IDFA/GAID) and limited device information to serve contextual ads. See AppLovin's Privacy Policy for full details. You can limit ad tracking at any time in your device settings.
  • Purchase validation — Purchase transactions are validated by RevenueCat. When you make or restore a purchase, RevenueCat receives receipt data (transaction ID, product identifier, platform, and price) solely to confirm your entitlement. No personal content from the app — no photos, notes, journal entries, or media — is shared with RevenueCat. See RevenueCat's Privacy Policy for full details.
  • Payment processing — Payment itself is handled entirely by Apple (App Store) or Google (Play Store). We do not receive or store your payment card details.
  • External APIs for app features — Where a specific feature requires an external API call (for example, fetching content or processing a request), only the minimum data necessary to fulfil that feature is sent. No personal photos, files, or journal content are ever transmitted as part of these calls.
ℹ️
What we never send over the internet

Regardless of the feature, we never transmit your photos, images, journal text, board content, goals, or any media you have added to any of our apps. Internet connections are used only for the specific, limited purposes listed above.

5A. Artificial Intelligence (AI) Features

Some of our apps include Artificial Intelligence (AI) features. Where supported, AI processing may occur entirely on your device using a local language model, or through a cloud AI provider that you choose.

AI-generated responses are provided for informational purposes and may be inaccurate or incomplete. Users should independently verify important information before relying on it.

5B. Bring Your Own Key (BYOK)

Some apps allow you to enter your own API key for supported AI providers. Your API keys are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to or stored on our servers. When you use a cloud AI provider, your requests are sent directly from your device to the provider you selected using your API key. We do not proxy, log, or retain your prompts or AI responses on our own servers.

6. Children's Privacy

Our apps are intended for users aged 13 and over. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information through one of our apps, please contact us and we will address it promptly.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time to reflect changes in our apps or applicable law. When we make a material change, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of any of our apps after a change is posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
